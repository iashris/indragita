##    17\. Day 6: The Alter-Ego You Need To Create 

## Explain this to your friend: 

"One of the challenges of living in a society is wanting to fit in. You need to develop an alter-ego that can protect your message from this natural inclination."

"You don't want to be invisible."

"These next questions are designed to remind you that a message is what will make you NOT be invisible."

"Imagine people are talking about you..." 

> **1\. How do they refer to you?**

> **2\. How do you want them to refer to you?**

Make your friend complete this sentence about other people talking about them:

"That’s the girl / guy who..."

"She / He changed my mind about..."

_Figure this out before moving on._

  


## **Who do you have to become to spread this message?**

Explain to your friend:

"Finding your message is 50% inspiration and 50% just a formal decision... the decision to amplify one small part of the person you are... And doing it for the next three years of your life."

"Start to separate yourself from the full dimensional person that you are... And visualize the singular dimensional person (brand / persona / identity) that you need to show to the world as the best vehicle for your message."

"David Goggins has Goggins"

"Gary Veynerchuck has Gary V"

"Like Batman says in 'Batman Begins'... You have to become A symbol to the people."

_FINALLY, ASK THEM: "What name would you give this new alter-ego of yours?"_
