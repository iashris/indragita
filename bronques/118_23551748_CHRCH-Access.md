##    15\. WORLD BUILDING (PART I): TURNING YOUR ART INTO A BRAND 

![](https://uploads.teachablecdn.com/attachments/Y3et8gBJTZymgF91tU5Q_WORLDBUILDING.JPG)

# **YOU ARE HERE**

You know when someone asks you what you're trying to achieve in the next few months, or years, or with your "career" in general... And your answer is kinda vague?

Let's solve that, please?

This is where you're at right now:

![](https://uploads.teachablecdn.com/attachments/laMi34OVRNO6pZfPptr6_WORLDBUILDINGdetail.PNG)

You have ideas.

You have followers.

And perhaps you don't know your message yet.

That's not uncommon.

But stubbornly, you still wonder "How can I grow from here?"

World Building is the most important concept for you to master when you're ready to build your career.

And one day, when you can afford a genius manager... You'll hire them to think about this stuff for you. That fast-talking magical being will work on expanding your BRAND ENERGY outward.

But until then... You'll have to do it yourself.

It's not so bad. It's actually a lot of fun once you realize it's creative.

World Building will focus your energy on what matters.

But if you don't know what your art is [(the product)](https://learn.lastnightsparty.tv/courses/enroll/lectures/22464200), or you haven't figured out your [audience psychographic](https://learn.lastnightsparty.tv/courses/enroll/lectures/9022531) or [your message](https://learn.lastnightsparty.tv/courses/enroll/lectures/23009474)... Then you need to go back to the earlier modules in The Mystery School and keep working until you achieve The Holy Trinity: Product, Audience and Message.

Or not.

Just keep in mind that I'm sharing the fastest route I've seen anybody use, and that I've used myself.

_World building is an advanced process for turning_ The Holy Trinity _(once you have it) into a unique, ever expanding, addictive EXPERIENCE for your fans._

Below is the complete journey for building a brand that makes a cultural impact.

  


# **BASE LEVEL: THE HOLY TRINITY**

Every artist or brand out there making a decent living year after year is operating at this base level.

Even if it's a weak version of it.

The best ones?

They have strong art... They're focusing on a niche audience... And they know their message in their sleep.

Everything that happens in an artist or brand's career is dependent on the synergy between these elements.

## PRODUCT (the ONE thing you provide): art / career / idiom / art form / Polaris Star

## AUDIENCE (those who are listening): customers / clients / psychographic / pocket of people

## MESSAGE (the aspirational worldview that makes you different): articulated in words / a new niche / a new sound / an artist blue ocean.

Your MESSAGE is the seed that will propel your art and audience relationship to a new level of sustainability and competition.

  


# **DESIRED LEVEL: WORLD BUILDING (EXPANDING BRAND ENERGY)**

While achieving The Holy Trinity is indeed a major accomplishment...

The ultimate goal is: "World Building" (Expanding Brand Energy).

World building is the gradual process of turning a product into an Exclusive Experience... An audience into a Fanbase... And your message into a Cultural Shift.

Here are the stages of each.

  


# **HOW YOUR ART BECOMES AN EXCLUSIVE 'EXPERIENCE'**

#   


# EXPERIENCE (BASE LEVEL): When your art takes on symbolic meaning for a special group of people. This is what happens when your product has connected with a group's identity.

The reasons for buying your art have changed now. Fans buy everything you do because they f-ck with your message.

Musicians: your albums, your collaborations (you're not just a hit song on a mixtape anymore, they want to hear everything you have to say)

Writers: they buy all your books

Artist: fans buy prints of your work online, they buy your artwork put on objects

Stylists: You are the final word on the subject. You are their thought leader. How your fans present themselves to the world is because of your influence.

They have bought into your worldview. They feel like they've connected to an important idea.

And to them, the products you sell are symbols of that worldview.

The experience you offer is high quality. But it isn't more expensive. It's available to everyone but only a few "understand".

And when they can't get enough of this first level experience...

That's what leads to the creation of the next level of "experience called:

  


# THE ENHANCED EXPERIENCE

Your art has so much impact on your fans that it creates an energy around your brand. This energy brings to life all sorts of spin offs.

For musicians: The live show and merch are the real money makers. They want to touch you.

Authors: Your book gets a movie deal... Your reading tours

Artists: Your original canvas in a gallery... Your collected works

Stylists: You have your own product line.

If you've taken the time to find your "artist blue ocean" (the combination of product and message that makes you one of a kind), your brand will fast-track what takes everyone else ages.

  


# THE EXCLUSIVE EXPERIENCE

This is a version of your experience only available to fans with deep pockets.

Musician: A private performance.

Writers: You're ASKED to write a book on a topic. You teach small groups of other established writers.

Artists: a commissioned work.

Stylists: Getting hired to do make-up/hair/styling for a celebrity exclusively...

Ironically, the more expensive the experience, the less "work" you have to do to deliver. That's because at this stage of your career, your "value" is more symbolic than it is effort based.

that generate more income for LESS work.

  


# **HOW YOUR AUDIENCE BECOMES AN INTER-WORLD FANBASE**

  


# FANS

This is the part of your audience that puts their money where their mouth is.

Alternative products are no longer an option for them.

You are the best version of your category to them.

  


# THE SUPERFANS

This is the segment of your FANS who not only love you, but are vocal about it.

They promote what you do so hard that they attracting more FANS even while you sleep.

  


# INTER-WORLD FANBASE:

This is when leaders of other WORLDS become your Superfans.

You know you've arrived when your peers want to introduce THEIR WORLD to YOUR WORLD. It's when the people who have built their own incredible worlds-- Start to recognize and admire yours.

Another sign you've reached INTER-WORLD FANDOM is when big corporations (AKA fans with deep pockets) are ready to pay you to associate THEIR world with YOURS (or at the very least they'll try to gain temporary access to your world). They want to endorse you. They wanna advertise with you. They're ready to pay for access to the eyes and ears of your audience, your fans, and your superfans. They will pay anything to be near you. They do this hoping your aura will somehow rub off on them

It's at this point that you can zoom out and see the world you have built...

Is part of a whole universe.

  


# **HOW YOUR MESSAGE BECOMES A CULTURE SHIFT**

  


First...

  


# With SLOGANS AND SYMBOLS.

Slogans and symbols start to emerge as your fans start to find shorthand to share your message between themselves. You and your fans have your own lingo. When your audience starts repeating the words and catchphrases you use like it's their own, you're getting there.

  


# Then a MOVEMENT.

When others start to share your message without prompting from you AND / OR when they start to gather in your name.

  


# And eventually a CULTURE SHIFT.

You message gets mass adoption. It's bigger than you now. Many people don't even realize that you were the catalyst. The majority of culture shares your worldview (that years ago was on the fringes, wow).

  


===

  


Okay so...

Maybe you're still at the IDEAS / FOLLOWERS / NO MESSAGE level...

That's ok. It's still good to glance at this module now to understand where you're heading.

But please go back and study 'The Book Of Purpose' and 'The Book Of Influence' so you can move forward on a solid foundation.

Use The Mystery School to help you elevate your "great ideas" into a powerful HOLY TRINITY of:

PRODUCT (YOUR ART) / AUDIENCE (RIDE OR DIES) / MESSAGE (VISION).

### _But if you've done the work and you believe you're already there... (!!!)_

And you want to expand your brand energy...

[You have to know how to create an experience.](https://learn.lastnightsparty.tv/courses/enroll/lectures/23575213)
