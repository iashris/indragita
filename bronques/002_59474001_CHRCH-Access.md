##    ++CHRCH Access 

Join your fellow artists...  


![](https://uploads.teachablecdn.com/attachments/Y7L78F3RnyMjKGmStP3W_NAME_LOGO_BLACK.png)

++CHURCH is the official online community for the 'Make Art Not Content' movement. It's where you get to apply everything you've learned in The Mystery School.

Here you will meet all sorts of artists who are on the same path as you.

But before you enter, I want to warn you…

++CHRCH isn’t like other online communities.

First of all, there’s no “like” button for fake validation. ++CHRCH is about the "reply" button. It's about engaging in real conversations with your peers.

Secondly, I DON’T want you to spend time in there every day. ++CHRCH is not Instagram. I want you to be CREATING ART during the week!

But on Sundays… That’s when we absolutely want to see your face 😇.

Come in and...

1\. Post your iterations / Share your art / Get feedback.

2\. Meet your future collaborators.

And, of course... Enjoy each week's SUNDAY SERMON (and all the archives)!

**use the same email as the one you use for The Mystery School or we won't find you**

(++CHRCH invites go out on Sundays, first-come, first-served ✞)
