##    10\. Every Artist Is A Renegade 

A blocked artist is simply an artist who has to be un-programmed.  


The problems start when every time we think about doing something bold, we go and consult whatever past programming we've received to decide whether to do it or not.  


But how often is that final decision be based on our authentic selves?  


**Have you been making art that is merely a collection of borrowed ideas?**  


Does your artistic vision only reflect the sum total of your upbringing… Your schooling… Your social grooming… Your culture… Your country’s laws… Your religion… Your desire to fit in…  


Ask yourself...

_How much of your art so far has come from your own authentic voice?_  


If you’ve been feeling rising anxiety creeping up on you… It’s probably because you’re not living your truth. It’s probably because you’re borrowing someone else’s truth. Every time you play the game, you deny yourself of your full expression.   


You go through life putting on masks going along with the crowd for your survival. And before you know it, everything you create is just a mirror of what society or the CULTure wants it to be.

**Society relies on people believing that the whole construct isn’t an old white man's fabrication.**  


But some people decide to untether from groupThink. They shed the masks. They stop being complicit.

It isn’t easy. But they do it for their spiritual survival.  


When that second awakening happens to you...  


_A renegade is born._  


![](https://uploads.teachablecdn.com/attachments/T41sLIR6R4GTz58iUCO1_Renegade.png)

There are renegades...   


And there are pretenders.

  


#  **1\. The Renegades**  


It's easy to spot a renegade.  


They’re the people who inspire us.  


They used to share the same beliefs as everybody else— But then something happened along the way, and they started going down their own path.  


We don't always know what made them change, but we love what they now stand for.   


**Renegades give us clues about what's possible.  **  


They come up with the concepts/ideas/philosophies that other people share.   


Maybe they experienced pain at some point, but now they've found an antidote. An antidote that the masses decry as blasphemy. An antidote that they know is the truth.  


Armed with their message, renegades are ready to jump over the rules to get where they gotta go.  


They take pleasure in not being a part of the mainstream because the mainstream feels so constricting and lame.  


Their clarity about why the conventional way of thinking is wrong, is such, that they'll gladly pay the price of not fitting in.  


And then…   


_They wait for the rest of the world to catch up._

  


#  **2\. The Pretenders**  


The Pretenders are the wannabes. Fake renegades.  


You know the type. All talk, no action.  


They wear the right clothes. They speak the right soundbites. But because they always disappoint, we end up doubting the real renegades who show up on our path.  


They muddy the water.   


So here are a few signs to spot them early…  


Pretenders are those people who 'steal' the inspiring concepts/ideas/philosophies that renegades come up with and try to pass them off as their own.   


Pretenders will use all of their energy to give off a false exterior of strength, but really… They're crumbling inside.  


They will say things like "I don't give a fuck," not realizing that if they REALLY didn't give a fuck they wouldn't even be thinking about it. They’d be vibrating at a higher energy.  


Another favorite pretender phrase is "maybe it wasn't meant to be”. They love this one because it gives them an easy way out.   


(Nothing great was "meant to be". The Mona Lisa wasn't "meant to be". The Taj Mahal wasn't "meant to be". The person you met on the plane that ended up becoming the Godmother to your newborn 2 years later wasn't "meant to be". )  


Things happen because renegades make them happen.  


**Pretenders' "acts of courage" are always tales of nostalgia.  **  


They always "already did that". They always "already went there"  


But when you look closely... they're just living off the fumes. They live cozy little lives... and judge the hell out of YOU for doing what they wished they could do.   


Pretenders secretly hate renegades for being amazing because it reminds them that they aren't.  


Pretenders are the people who will "ghost" you when it’s time for revolutionary action.  


Pretenders will justify their failures. They will take the easy way out while making it seem like strength. And they'll sell you "the dream" in the most poetic way.  


Pretenders talk so loud they fool you into thinking they're renegades. They muddy the water.   


_Expose the pretenders. Call them out._

  


#  **3\. Renegades in training.**  


I lied. There’s a third group.  


And they're just as inspiring as the renegades are.  


They are the “renegades in training”.   


The "renegades in training" are those who are trying to improve themselves every day. They haven’t overhauled their whole thinking yet but… They ARE looking for any opportunity they can find to express a new side of themselves.   


They are constantly shedding old skin.  


For example… They know they're shy. But being shy doesn't stop them from acting like extroverts… In the name of their art.  


**And they know they're scared. But being scared doesn't stop them from acting courageous when they see injustice.**  


They’re renegades in training because they’re still looking for their world view.  


But as they’re searching, they already know three things for sure…  


1\. That you're either expanding… Or you're contracting.

2\. That if you're "not busy living… Then you're busy dying" (Bob Dylan).  


But best of all…  


 _They know that finding how to express their truth is just an iteration away._

  


#  **4\. Retired Renegades**  


Yeah sorry... Totally one last one.  


The Retired Renegades  


Retired Renegades don't have the energy for the renegade stuff anymore.  


Either that or their responsibilities have yanked them out of the game.  


But it doesn’t mean they aren’t making a worthy contribution.  


They’re the ones who “finance” the active renegades with money, power, connections, and best of all…  


**Hidden knowledge.**  


They're the "supportive friends" who rail against the haters.   


They are the subversive teachers… Who may look like they’re leading a normal life (to the outside world), but their whole aim is to infuse the next generation with renegade ideology.  


They are the receptionist or secretary who secretly "unlocks the office door" so that the renegades can get in and do some renegade shit.  


Retired Renegades aren’t doing wild things on the front lines anymore but… they're busy helping renegades infiltrate the system.   


Retired Renegades may now be “quietly raising children”. But believe me… They're not raising ordinary children. They're raising mini-renegades. They're indoctrinating their offspring with renegade values.   


And we thank them for that.  


So tell me…  


Are you a renegade?  


A renegade in training?  


Or a retired renegade?  


'Cause I know there are no "pretenders" in here.  


But truth be told:  


**We all have a little pretender inside of us trying to run the show. The only way to suffocate that voice is to dive deep into renegade territory. ’That’s how you give that insidious voice no room to rise.**

So, my fellow renegades, I end this by asking you…  


 _What "acts of courage” are next on your horizon?_
