##    12\. Day 3 (PART I): Call A Friend 

Today is one of the funnest days of The Drill Down Method.

Today you will ask the student to call a friend.

Preferably their BEST friend.

Put them on speakerphone.

Have the student ask their best friend these questions:

(and make notes of the answers)

> **1\. How do you see me?**

> **2\. What do I not shut up about? What am I obsessed with?**

> **3\. What's my message to the world?**

> **4\. What's the light I bring to the world?**
