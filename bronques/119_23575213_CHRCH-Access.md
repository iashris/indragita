##    16\. WORLD BUILDING (PART II): HOW TO CREATE YOUR POWERFUL ONLINE EXPERIENCE 

_*Where can you take your audience to, that they will absolutely love, but that they’ve never been to before?_

If you can answer this question successfully, you may have a million dollar brand.

Use this module to ask yourself the questions that will help you create an online experience that enhances people’s lives.

World Building is about making everything that you do online mirror the values and principles of your “real world” art or personal philosophy. 

Every decision you make should be measured against: “Are we getting our audience closer to (insert whatever your ultimate experience is here)?”

Your experience should have extremely high exchange value (IE. be worth a lot of money - even if only on paper right now).

Build an experience of interconnected parts. (Every move you do online is connected to the other pieces of the puzzle.)

Make people say:

“What the fuck is (insert your brand name here)?????"

"Who the fuck is (insert personal brand here)???"

"I want to be a part of that world! I want to hang out with those people!”

Make it so powerful that even celebrities will want to get in on the action.

  


# **QUESTIONS YOU NEED TO ASK YOURSELF TO MAKE THE ANSWERS COME TRUE**

How do you want your product/experience/world to be seen by others? (Positioning Question #1)

How do you want yourself to be seen by others? (Positioning Question #2)

What are the repetitive parts of your world?

What are the "serial" aspects of your world that keeps them coming back?

Who are the reoccurring characters?

What are your "catchy" characteristic traits?

Your goal is to create an eyebrow-raising world until it blows up.

But that won't happen until you know what that world is.

That's why you have to answer these questions right now.

And If you're intentional and clear about it... It will happen. It's law. 

It has to become your Polaris Star and the only thing on your mind.

Especially when you feel friction.

Purposely keep doing all the things your haters hate but that your Superfans cherish.

This will raise your exchange value until it's through the roof.

Now, it isn't just fun and games anymore. 

It's a business.
